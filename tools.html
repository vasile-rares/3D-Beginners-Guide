3D BEGINNER'S GUIDE

Looking for 3D design software?

Check out this guide to find out which is the best 3D modeling software for your own needs

BLENDER

Free, open-source 3D creation software Used in animation, game design, architecture, manufacturing, and product design, 3D models play a crucial role in every kind of production — both digital and physical. Designing these 3D models can seem even more mystical than traditional art forms, but after some practice with the powerful 3D modeling software behind them, it’s all really quite straightforward.

Read More →

ZBRUSH

Digital sculpting software Quite possibly the most advanced digital sculpting modeler on this list, ZBrush is the primary offering from Pixologic. In 2022, the company was acquired by fellow 3D software solution provider Maxon, the company behind Cinema4D. ZBrush is generally the favored tool among digital artists who prefer working with “digital clay” over mesh or parametric modeling.

Read More →

CINEMA 4D

Motion graphics software Maxon’s animation powerhouse with advanced simulation capabilities, Cinema 4D, is often one of the programs behind the most mesmerizing visual effects. In fact, Cinema 4D’s capabilities are such that it’s used in VFX-heavy Hollywood movies. Pacific Rim and Tron: Legacy are two examples of the photorealistic fantasy you can accomplish with Cinema 4D.

Read More →

AUTODESK MAYA

Industry-leading 3D animation and modeling software An industry standard for a large part of its 23-year lifespan, Autodesk Maya is a powerful 3D animation software used by industry professionals for high-quality visual effects and motion graphics.

Read More →

ADOBE SUBSTANCE 3D PAINTER

Texture painting software for highly realistic results Adobe Substance Painter is a 3D painting software that enables artists to create highly detailed and realistic textures for 3D models. With Substance Painter, artists can easily create textures for a wide range of surfaces, such as metal, skin, cloth, and more. The software also supports advanced features like real-time ray tracing and 4K rendering, making it a popular choice for game developers, VFX artists, and other 3D professionals.

Read More →

MARMOSET TOOLBAG

Real-time rendering software for 3D artists Marmoset Toolbag is a powerful real-time rendering software designed for 3D artists and game developers. It offers a range of advanced features, including a physically-based rendering (PBR) system, real-time lighting and shadowing, and advanced material editing tools. Marmoset Toolbag supports a wide range of file formats and platforms, making it a versatile and flexible tool for creating high-quality, photorealistic renderings of 3D models.

Read More →

UNREAL ENGINE

Game engine for creating interactive experiences Unreal Engine is a powerful game engine that enables developers to create immersive and interactive experiences for a variety of platforms, including PC, console, mobile, and virtual reality. The engine provides a range of tools and features for creating high-quality graphics, advanced physics simulations, and complex game logic. With its user-friendly interface and extensive documentation, Unreal Engine is a popular choice for indie developers and AAA studios alike. Additionally, the engine offers a large community of developers and support resources, making it an excellent choice for anyone looking to get started with game development.
